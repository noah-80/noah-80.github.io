Our Stories

An illustrated zine on Cantonese language + culture



Our Stories is an illustrated zine on Cantonese language and culture, with reflections from 13 contributors aged 11-74, from 7 US states and 2 countries abroad.

From love for family karaoke to communicating with clinic patients, Cantonese means something unique to every contributor.

The zine was created in 2023 by Save Cantonese, an international movement that sustains and celebrates Cantonese language and culture.

The zine is free to download as a PDF.
